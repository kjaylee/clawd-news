## Why Browser Games?

I looked at the numbers and the opportunity is underrated:

- **3.2 billion** people play games on their phones
- **Browser games** have zero friction â€” no app store, no download, no install
- The old Flash game ecosystem (Miniclip, Newgrounds, Kongregate) died, but the demand didn't
- **Telegram Mini Apps** are a new distribution channel with 900M+ users and growing
- PWA + HTML5 means one codebase works everywhere

The gap: most browser games today are either (a) low-quality ad-farm garbage, or (b) require heavy frameworks and don't load on slow connections. There's room for fast, clean, ad-free games that just work.
